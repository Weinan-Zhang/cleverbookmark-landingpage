Why Use CleverBookmark

We Provide:

1. The tab won't be added to bookmark until you decide to add it.

2. The title and folder are editable the first time you open 'CleverBookmark'.

3. Creating a new folder or selecting an existing folder are out-of-box options.

4. Intelligent hint feature helps you locate your desired folder faster.

5. Pin your frequently used bookmarks to add them more efficiently.

6. Provide options to add your bookmark on the top/bottom of the folder.

Addressed Issues:

1. The tab will be added to the last used folder.

2. Title and folder can only be edited by clicking the 'edit' button.

3. New folders can only be created by clicking the 'choose another folder' option.

4. Existing folders can't be filtered as you type.

5. Existing folders are not pinnable.

6. Can't add bookmarks to the top of the folder.
